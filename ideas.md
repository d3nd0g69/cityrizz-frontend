**Approach 1: Urban Broadsheet**
- **Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalist Editorial
- **Core Principles**: Raw typographic hierarchy, high-contrast black/white with a single punchy accent, asymmetric column grids, zero decorative chrome
- **Color Philosophy**: Near-black (#111) backgrounds for hero, white body, and a single electric red (#E8001D) for category labels and CTAs — evoking classic alt-weekly print energy
- **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric 3-column masonry on homepage; full-bleed hero with overlaid bold headline; sidebar-heavy inner pages
- **Signature Elements**: Thick black border rules between sections; all-caps condensed category slugs; oversized pull-quotes
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Instant, no-frills hover states — underlines snap in, images scale 1.02x, no easing theatrics
- **Animation**: Section entrance via translateY(20px) → 0 on scroll; no parallax; fast 150ms transitions
- **Typography System**: Bebas Neue (display/headlines) + Source Serif 4 (body) + IBM Plex Mono (metadata/dates)
</text>
